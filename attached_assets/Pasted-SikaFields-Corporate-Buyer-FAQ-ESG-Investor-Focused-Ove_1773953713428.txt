SikaFields – Corporate Buyer FAQ (ESG & Investor Focused)
🏢 Overview
1. What is SikaFields?

SikaFields is a climate technology platform that enables organizations to invest in high-quality, nature-based carbon removal projects while delivering measurable environmental and social impact in emerging markets.

2. What problem does SikaFields solve?

Many carbon offset solutions lack:

Transparency

Traceability

Real community impact

SikaFields addresses this by connecting buyers directly to verified, farmer-led projects, ensuring credible carbon removal and measurable ESG outcomes.

🌱 Carbon Credits & ESG Alignment
3. What types of carbon credits does SikaFields offer?

We primarily offer nature-based carbon removal credits, including:

Agroforestry

Reforestation and afforestation

Regenerative agriculture

Soil carbon sequestration

We also support energy transition projects, such as energy-efficient cookstoves.

4. How do SikaFields credits align with ESG frameworks?

SikaFields supports:

Environmental (E): Verified CO₂ removal and biodiversity restoration

Social (S): Increased farmer income, job creation, community development

Governance (G): Transparent MRV processes, audit trails, and reporting

5. Can SikaFields credits support net-zero commitments?

Yes. Our credits are suitable for:

Voluntary carbon offsetting

Scope 3 emissions reduction strategies

Net-zero transition plans (as a complementary measure, not a substitute for reduction)

🔍 Verification, Quality & Risk
6. How are carbon credits verified?

SikaFields applies a rigorous MRV (Monitoring, Reporting, Verification) framework:

Scientific carbon estimation models

Field data collection and validation

Remote sensing (where applicable)

Independent third-party verification (where required)

7. How do you ensure credit quality?

All credits are designed to meet key integrity principles:

Additionality: Projects would not occur without carbon financing

Permanence: Long-term carbon storage (e.g., 30-year commitments)

No double-counting: Unique registry and retirement system

Leakage control: Monitoring to prevent emissions shifting elsewhere

8. What risks should buyers be aware of?

Like all nature-based solutions:

Carbon sequestration takes time

Environmental risks (e.g., drought, fire) may impact outcomes

Verification timelines can be long

SikaFields mitigates these through:

Conservative estimation models

Ongoing monitoring

Diversified project portfolios
